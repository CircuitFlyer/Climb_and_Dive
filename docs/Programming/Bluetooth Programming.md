## Bluetooth Programming Instructions ##

| Motor RPM | 3500 to 15000 | 10000 |
| Climb Gain | 0 to 10 | 5 |
| Dive Gain | 0 to 10 | 5 |
| Motor Acceleration | 1 to 10 | 5 |
| Number of Motor Poles | 2 to 20 | 14 |
